=== PARKOUR MASTER ===

Price: $3.99

LORE:
The city rooftops are yours to conquer. A mysterious architect has built 50 courses across the skyline — each one harder than the last. Only the swift and precise will reach the top.

=== FEATURES ===

5 DIFFICULTY TIERS (10 levels each):
  Novice:     2-block gaps, slow pistons, basic jumping
  Skilled:    3-block gaps, moving platforms, ladder climbs
  Advanced:   Ice paths, timed jumps, trap avoidance
  Expert:     Neo jumps (4+ blocks), 180s, wall runs
  Master:     Elytra courses, slime launchers, zero-error gauntlets

CHECKPOINT SYSTEM:
  - Auto-checkpoints every 3-5 levels
  - Manual checkpoint reset with /checkpoint set
  - Glass checkpoint pillars glow when activated
  - /checkpoint list shows all earned checkpoints

TIME TRIALS:
  - Each level tracks personal best
  - /pb shows your 10 fastest completions
  - Gold (top 10%), Silver (top 30%), Bronze (top 60%) medals
  - Global leaderboard for each difficulty tier

UNLOCKABLE COSMETICS:
  - Trail particles: Flame, Soul Speed, Sparkle, Dragon Breath
  - Jump Effects: Double Jump, Spring Boots, Aura Jump
  - Title colors: Gold, Aqua, Red, Rainbow
  - Victory dances: Fireworks, Lightning, Explosion
