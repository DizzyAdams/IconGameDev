=== MEDIEVAL KINGDOM ===

Price: $4.99

LORE:
King Alaric has fallen. The realm is fractured — villages burn, dungeons overflow with monsters, and dark forces gather at the borders. As the new lord, you must rebuild, recruit allies, and defend the kingdom from the upcoming siege.

=== FEATURES ===

KINGDOM BUILDING:
  - Place your first campfire to claim the land
  - Build structures: Wall, Tower, Keep, Market, Stable
  - Each building unlocks new NPCs and recipes
  - Upgrade buildings with resources and gold
  - Unlock banners, flags, and decorative blocks

QUEST GIVER NPCS:
  - Sir Aldric (Main Quests): Progress the storyline
  - Master Builder (Side Quests): Gather building materials
  - Alchemist (Dailies): Potions, rare ingredients
  - Bard (Exploration): Discover hidden locations
  - Each NPC has 5-10 unique quests with escalating rewards

TRADER SYSTEM:
  - Wandering Merchant: Random rare trades
  - Blacksmith: Tools, weapons, armor for iron/gold
  - Farmer: Food trades, animal breeding
  - Librarian: Enchanted books, maps, paper
  - Trader reputation system: better trades with loyalty

DUNGEON RAIDS:
  - 5 dungeons: Goblin Caves, Skeleton Catacombs, Spider Nests,
    Zombie Crypts, Dragon Lair (final boss)
  - Each dungeon has 3-5 rooms with loot chests
  - Boss fights with unique mechanics and drops
  - Co-op mode: up to 4 players

CASTLE DEFENSE EVENTS:
  - Waves of enemies attack the castle at night
  - 10 waves per event, increasing difficulty
  - Build defenses: Walls, moats, arrow towers, lava pits
  - Boss wave every 5th night
  - Scoreboard tracks kills and surviving villagers
