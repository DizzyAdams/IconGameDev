# Store Listing Metadata Template (Partner Center)

One block per pack, per locale. Fields match Partner Center offer metadata.
Keep EN always; add PT-BR for our priority market.

## EN (default)

```
Pack directory: <dir-name>
Title (<=60):    <Pack Name>
Description (100-300 chars):
  <Pack Name> brings N amazing <skins/textures/world> to your Minecraft
  Bedrock world! <One-line highlight>. Perfect for <audience>.>
Search terms (5): <term1>, <term2>, <term3>, <term4>, <term5>
Category:         <Skin Pack | Texture Pack | World | Mashup>
Age rating:       E / E10+
Price tier:       <160 | 310 | 440 | 800 | 1500 | 3500 | 7000> Minecoins
```

## Rules (from 04 + 05 + 08)

- First 100 chars must hook the buyer.
- No "on sale"/price words, no external links, no "rate 5 stars".
- Screenshots: 4–6, 1280x720, actual in-game content.
- Icon 256x256 PNG; hero 1920x1080.
- Title must match `manifest.json` header name.
